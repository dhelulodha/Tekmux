TEKMUX


TEKMUX is an Web-Andro-Electro Hackathon event to be conducted by Aakash Research Labs on 11-12 August. Something which separates this from all the other events is the fact that for the first time in SRM an Electronic Hackathon, Web Hackathon and Android Hackathon will be conducted.
Apart from these 3 main events and project exhibition will also be conducted.
We here at ARL support and promote digital India and cashless India and to move forward with this initiative we are going to conduct a Digital India Campaign as a Pre-TEKMUX event.
Students from different government schools around our area will be invited and they will be briefed about the fun science as a subject carry and also how to digital will also be promoted and taught to them.
