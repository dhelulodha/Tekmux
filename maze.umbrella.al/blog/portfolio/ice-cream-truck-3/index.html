DROIDHAX

An Android Hackathon exploring the efficiency of participants to develop applications.Members Per Team: 1-2 people

Round I: Debugging

TIME: 15 Minutes

Each contestant shall be given 10 debugging Questions.

JUDGING CRITERIA: Each question will have certain points according to the difficulty level.

Round II: Adding Features

TIME: 30 Minutes

Each team will be provided with a basic android app. Then the team needs to pick a particular feature via lucky draw system, which they would have to implement in the app.

JUDGING CRITERIA: The efficiency of the features and quality of the code.

Round III: Client Interaction

TIME: 3 Hours

The team will meet a prospective client who will give the requirements and teams need to develop a fully functioning app from the scratch with all necessary features working and then sell it to the client.

JUDGING CRITERIA: Efficiency of the app, working of all features, bugs in the code and in case of tie, time can be considered.
